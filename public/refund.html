Refund Policy

Last updated: 16 April 2025  ·  2B Connected Ltd

All purchases of Aethelgard are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited, who acts as Merchant of Record. Refund requests are handled by Paddle's support team on our behalf.

1. How Refunds Work

Because Paddle is the Merchant of Record for all Aethelgard transactions, Paddle collects your payment and issues your receipt. Refunds are therefore processed by Paddle and credited back to your original payment method.

To request a refund, contact Paddle's buyer support directly or email us at contact@aethelgard.finance and we will initiate the request on your behalf.

2. 14-Day Right of Withdrawal (UK and EU Consumers)

If you are a consumer in the United Kingdom or European Union, you ordinarily have the right to withdraw from a purchase of digital content within 14 days of the transaction date, without giving a reason, under the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013 (UK) and Directive 2011/83/EU (EU).

However, under those same regulations, you lose the right of withdrawal in respect of digital content if you have expressly consented to delivery beginning before the end of the 14-day period and acknowledged that the right of withdrawal is thereby lost. Aethelgard licence keys are delivered immediately upon payment completion. By completing a purchase you consent to immediate delivery and acknowledge that this results in the loss of your right of withdrawal.

Notwithstanding the above, we will consider refund requests made within 14 days on a case-by-case basis where the Software is defective or was materially misrepresented.

3. Annual Licences

Refund requests for annual licences will be considered if:

  • the licence key was not delivered within 24 hours of payment; or
  • the Software contains a material defect that we are unable to resolve within a reasonable time; or
  • the purchase was made in error and the licence key has not been activated.

We do not offer pro-rata refunds for unused time on an annual licence once the key has been activated.

4. Lifetime Licences

Lifetime licences are non-refundable once the licence key has been delivered, except where required by applicable law or where the Software is found to be materially defective and we are unable to provide a remedy.

5. Duplicate Purchases

If you accidentally purchased the same licence tier twice, contact us at contact@aethelgard.finance within 7 days. Duplicate charge refunds are processed promptly.

6. Statutory Rights

Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 (UK) or equivalent applicable consumer protection legislation. If the Software is not of satisfactory quality, fit for purpose, or as described, you may be entitled to a remedy regardless of the provisions above.

7. How to Request a Refund

Email contact@aethelgard.finance with:

  • the email address used at purchase;
  • your Paddle order or transaction reference (shown on your receipt); and
  • a brief description of your reason for requesting a refund.

We aim to respond within 2 business days. If approved, Paddle will process the refund within 5–10 business days depending on your payment method.
