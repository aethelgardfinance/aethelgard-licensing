Last updated: 16 April 2025  ·  2B Connected Ltd

This Privacy Notice explains how 2B Connected Ltd ("we", "us", "our"), registered in England and Wales, collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you purchase or use Aethelgard software. We are the data controller for the purposes of UK GDPR.

1. Data We Collect and Why

Data Source Purpose Legal basis
Email address, name Collected by Paddle at checkout; passed to us on purchase completion Deliver your licence key; provide purchase-related support Performance of a contract (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))
Licence key and tier Generated by us on purchase Validate your software licence; reissue keys on request Performance of a contract (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))
Transaction ID Paddle webhook notification Reconcile payments; handle refund or dispute queries Legitimate interests (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))
Support correspondence Emails you send us Respond to your enquiries and resolve issues Legitimate interests (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))

We do not collect payment card details. All payment data is collected and held by Paddle; see Paddle's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the purposes set out in the table above. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Processors

We use the following processors who act on our instructions under data processing agreements:

  • Paddle.com Market Limited — payment processing and Merchant of Record. Paddle independently collects and processes your billing data as a controller in its own right. Paddle Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery. Your email address and licence key are transmitted to Resend solely to deliver your purchase confirmation. Resend Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel Inc. — cloud infrastructure on which our backend runs. Vercel may process request metadata (IP address, timestamps) in the ordinary course of hosting. Vercel Privacy Policy.

4. International Transfers

Resend and Vercel are US-based services. Where your personal data is transferred outside the UK, we rely on appropriate safeguards — including the UK's International Data Transfer Agreements (IDTAs) or equivalent standard contractual clauses — as required by UK GDPR.

5. Retention

We retain your email address and associated transaction records for as long as your licence is active, and for up to 7 years thereafter, to comply with UK financial record-keeping obligations. Support correspondence is retained for 3 years from the date of the last exchange or until you request deletion, whichever is sooner.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Restriction — ask us to restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format where technically feasible
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@aethelgard.finance. We will respond within one month.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at any time.

7. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies. No cookies are set by us on aethelgard.finance. The Software itself does not phone home or transmit usage data.

8. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice periodically. We will post the revised version on this page with an updated date. Material changes will be notified to registered licence holders by email.
